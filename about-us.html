About Us

Welcome to your Wellness and Health Partner, N.U.T.R.I. App (Nutritional Utility for Transformative Recipe Innovator).

The N.U.T.R.I. App helps users track their daily nutrition and make healthier food choices. We believe that good nutrition is the key to living a happy and balanced life.

This app provides a user-friendly platform for individuals to input their daily food intake and track calories, macronutrients, and micronutrients. Staying healthy can be challenging, but we are committed to supporting your wellness journey with accurate tools and guidance.

Thank you for choosing the N.U.T.R.I. App as your companion toward better nutrition and well-being.
